    Take a Walkthrough

    TribeArc is an investment community and business funding platform for business professionals and investors to pool funds with the goal of funding the development of cooperative business and enterprises, creating employment opportunities while also guaranteeing return on investment. We provide startup financing in whole or in part on the need assessment and provide expert assistance and mentoring in the area of leadership and managerial skills to ensure the survival, sustainability and expansion of the cooperative and its business enterprises.

    How it works

    TribeArc works on the principle of voluntary savings, loans, grants, investment and dividend sharing. It is not affected by the entry and exit of members save money collectively, periodically and voluntarily.
